Lodging

Emily and Charlie have noted a few options nearby in the event that guests would like to stay at a hotel.

  • Austin Marriott North (Round Rock) is a fifteen minute drive to the ceremony location.

  • La Quinta Inn & Suites Austin North/Round Rock is a 10-15 minute drive from the couple's home and is pet-friendly.

  • Airbnb is an option to find a rental in Austin, Round Rock, or Pflugerville.

There are plenty of other hotels in the area, and Central Austin is only a 20 minute drive from the couple's home if you'd prefer to stay there.
