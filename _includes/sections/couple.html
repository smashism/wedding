Emily and Charlie

Emily Kausalik, a native of the Chicago suburbs in Illinois, and Charlie Whittle, a native of Texas, met in Austin, TX, in March 2013. Their first date was appropriately Austin-like, involving tacos from a local food truck and a night of playing games at Pinballz.

It didn't take long for Emily and Charlie to realize that they shared a lot of interests. They quickly bonded over a love of animals, comics, movies, and music (lots and lots of music). Soon they were spending every minute together.

In early 2014, Emily and Charlie got their first place together, along with their critters Lil (dog), Charlie (cat, now often referred to as Kittybear), and Ronnie (cat).

After Emily graduated with her PhD in Music Theory from UT-Austin she settled into a career in IT, and now works for The Home Depot's Austin Technology Center as a systems engineer managing Apple technology. Charlie is Pharmacy Manager at People's Rx, a local holistic and compounding pharmacy based out of Austin.

Emily and Charlie bought their first home together in June 2017, love their jobs, and are looking forward to starting the next chapter of their lives together.
