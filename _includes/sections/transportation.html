Traveling to Austin

Austin is located in Central Texas, with easy access from I-35, 290, and 71.

If you're driving from the north via I-35 North, take exit for W Louis Henna Blvd/I-45 and turn left on Louis Henna at the light. Turn right onto A.W. Grimes Blvd, then left on Pflugerville Parkway. Take a left on Heatherwilde, another left on Worley Drive, and a final left on Catumet Dr. The home will be on the right/north side of the road.

If you're planning to fly in, the closest airport is Austin-Bergstrom International Airport. The airport is about a forty-five minute drive from the couple's home and you will likely need to rent a car to get to Austin/Pflugerville (as there is unfortunately no regular shuttle or bus service that goes near the ceremony location).
