Registry

Charlie and Emily have been living together for a few years now, and have acquired most essential household goods in that time, so they were somewhat reluctant to register for gifts… until they discovered Zola registries. The online registry is non-conventional: it includes options like contributing to a life fund, or gifting an experience.

For guests who prefer to give a material gift the couple has traditional registries at Target and Amazon but please know that any heirloom, hand-made, or otherwise unique gifts are absolutely appreciated!

If you will be giving a registry item acquired through a different platform, you may send an email to Emily and request that a gift be marked as 'purchased' on Zola.
